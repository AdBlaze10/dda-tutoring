Contact / Sign-Up

To keep onboarding simple and reliable, DDA Tutoring uses Google Forms for parent sign-up.

Open Sign-Up Form

Google Forms Integration Setup

  1. Create a new Google Form at forms.google.com.
  2. Add the required fields: Parent Name, Student Name, Grade, Email, Phone, Goals/Concerns, Current resources, and Why DDA Tutoring?
  3. Click Send in Google Forms:
    • For a button link: copy the form URL and replace google_form_url in tutoring_site/views.py.
    • For inline embed: select the <> embed option and replace google_embed_url in tutoring_site/views.py.
  4. Open the Responses tab in Google Forms and click Link to Sheets to send all submissions automatically to Google Sheets.
